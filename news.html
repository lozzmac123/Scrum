Burberry Bar in the news

"We're CRACKERS for Burberry Bar's yearly wine and cheese fest this year!"

Burberry Bar's yearly wine and cheese fest is coming up and you simply have to make it this year. This year promises a even wider assortment of quality wine from vineyards across the globe. Also including Cheeses from Emmental, to Wensleydale. It's a event any wine lover, or cheese lover wouldn't want to miss. Last year saw the wine a cheese pairing competition won by Wallace from west yorkshire , for his paring of Cranberry infused Stilton, with a classic 40 year old Sauvignon Blanc. Mark your Calendars for the 13th of december so you're sure not to miss this Yuletide event!

Burberry Bar's 2017 Cancer Research fundrasing dinner has hit a new landmark!

Cancer Research funding is at a all time high, and Burberry Bar is helping contribute. "Cancer research dinners and charity dinners are a common thing here at Burberry Bar" says the owner, "We help accomidate charities for no extra cost, and contribute 20% of our earnings on the night to the charity hosting the event." The next charity event is for cancer research, and their are limited tickets on sale. It's a good cause readers, so get on dining!

Burberry Bar's first Michelin Star!

Latley, the restaurant Burberry Bar in Bournemouth has been awarded its first ever Michelin Star. Since its opening in 2015 Burberry Bar has been a unique and new establishment for anyone out in the town looking for a place to dine, and or drink. But just five days ago it was inspected and determined to be of a high enough stardard to be awarded the coverted Michelin Star. "We here at Burberry Bar are happy to be making such rapid progress. In two years We've got ourself a star that other restaurants somtimes get 15 years after its opening" Says the owner of Burberry Bar.
